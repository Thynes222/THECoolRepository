This is the title screen of one of my favorite Sonic games, and the first game I remember playing. Sonic 2! The game was originally released for the Sega Genesis in 1992, but I played it for the first time in around 2014, using the mobile port made by Christian Whitehead. As a kid, I played this game for hours on an Amazon Fire Kindle, because that's all it was released on that I had access to at the time. I was never very good at it, but I almost made it to the final 3 stages.
